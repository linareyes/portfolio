Focus Statement

My goal is to become a photorealist. I have found a great fascination for the detail of the face in all sorts of medium. Many of my photography portfolios consist of portraitures and many of my photographs include close ups of the face to focus on intricate details regarding the eyes, the nose, and the mouth. Apart from being a photographer, I am also a traditional artist which gives me the opportunity to explore the human face on an even deeper level. It means exploring the muscles and skull or anything that has to do with the anatomy of the face. I enjoy drawing from observations which gives me the practice I need to further perfect my skills.
